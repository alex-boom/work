You create the software,
we’ll give it wings

Napatech Pandion is a fast, reliable out-of-the-box engine for real-time capture, indexing and search of network data. Built for OEMs and enterprises, Pandion provides all the power and disk space you need to perfectly drive your expert piece of the appliance – the analysis software.

Ready to fly – right out of the box

Pandion comprises pre-integrated, pre-tested stream-to-disk hardware, based on industry-leading Napatech accelerator technology and Dell servers. Just add your analysis software and take off.

Support you can claw into

Pandion capture to disk supports industry-standard Berkeley Packet Filter (BPF) for “queries and search”, industry-standard PCAP applications and remote management
analysis.
